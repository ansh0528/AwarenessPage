About International Day of Happiness

Happiness is not just a feeling; it’s a way of life. The International Day of Happiness is dedicated to promoting global well-being, kindness, and positive change. InAmigos Foundation invites you to be part of this celebration, where we explore the science of happiness and how small actions can make a big impact on our lives and society.

Heightened Creativity
Prosocial Behavior
Enhanced Memory and Learning
Reduced Burnout

Why Happiness Matters

Physical Health

People who report higher levels of happiness are linked to lower stress levels, improved cardiovascular health, and a longer life expectancy.

Better Career

According to Happiness Matters, psychological well-being is strongly tied to better professional trajectories, including stronger cognitive performance, faster skill acquisition, and greater earning potential.

Stronger Relationships

Emotions are contagious. Being content and happy enables you to be a more compassionate person, ultimately deepening your bonds and improving your social support network.

How You Can Be Happy
